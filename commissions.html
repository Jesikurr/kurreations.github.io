Custom Commissions

Want something weird, nerdy, or completely unique? I got you!

How It Works:

  1. Submit Your Idea – Tell me what you’d like (plushies, figures, accessories, etc.).
  2. Design & Approval – I’ll send sketches or mockups for your approval.
  3. Crafting Begins – Once confirmed, I’ll start crocheting your creation.
  4. Final Touches & Delivery – Once finished, I’ll ship it to you!
