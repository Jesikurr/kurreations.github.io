About Kurreations

Hey there, fellow chaos enthusiast! I'm Jesikurr, the mastermind (or mad scientist?) behind Kurreations— where crochet meets creativity, chaos, and a little bit of geeky magic.

I don't just make cute plushies and cozy crafts—I conjure up weird, nerdy, and sometimes downright unhinged creations. Want a cuddly Cthulhu? A crocheted dragon with battle scars? A chunky goblin plush? Oh, you're in the right place.
